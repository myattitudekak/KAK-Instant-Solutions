Our Story
Born from a simple belief — everyone deserves a clean home.

KAK Cleaning Services was founded in 2019 by a team of passionate professionals who believed that high-quality home cleaning should not be a luxury. We started in Tirupati with just two team members and a handful of loyal customers.

What set us apart from day one was our commitment to machine-powered cleaning. While others relied on manual methods, we invested in professional-grade equipment that delivers results you can actually see and feel — in every corner, every tile, every surface.

Today, we serve over 1,200 happy families and businesses across Tirupati and surrounding areas. Our team has grown to 18+ trained professionals, each background-verified and certified in safe, eco-friendly cleaning practices.
